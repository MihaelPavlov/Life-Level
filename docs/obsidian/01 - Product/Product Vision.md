# Product Vision

> **"Train in the real world → progress in a game world."**

## The one-line pitch

Life-Level turns real fitness effort into RPG progression: every workout awards XP, stats, and in-game movement. You level up a character, explore a map, fight bosses, and compete with friends — *by going for a run*.

## Working titles

- Fitness RPG
- LifeLevel
- RealQuest

## Target user

Someone who:
- Already works out (or wants to) but struggles with consistency.
- Likes games — especially RPGs, progression systems, achievements.
- Would be motivated by **unlocking content** rather than raw health metrics alone.
- Owns a fitness tracker (Strava, Garmin, Apple Watch) or an Android phone with Health Connect.

## Core value proposition

Three things fused into one product:

1. **Fitness tracker** (Strava-like) — workouts logged or imported
2. **RPG game** (WoW-like) — character, stats, levels, ranks, titles, gear
3. **Social network** — friends, leaderboards, guilds, challenges

The USP is the **integration**: no existing app does all three well. Strava tracks but doesn't "reward." Zombies Run is gamified but narrow. Life-Level is a full progression game with real fitness as the fuel.

## Business model: freemium

| Tier | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Free** | Core features: character, activities, map, daily quests, basic bosses |
| **Premium** | Advanced stats, more quests, cosmetic items, premium customisation, seasonal cosmetics |

No pay-to-win. Premium is cosmetic and convenience. The game stays fair.

## Why it works (psychological levers)

- **Variable reward** — quests, chest drops, XP storms create gambling-grade retention loops.
- **Streaks + shields** — loss aversion retains daily engagement.
- **Rank ladder** — public prestige (titles, achievements) drives longer-term motivation.
- **Social comparison** — friends + leaderboards add accountability.
- **Delayed gratification** — level requirements for zones + items make fitness effort feel *earned*.

## Non-goals (explicit)

- **Not a medical app.** No diet tracking, no calorie-counting for weight loss, no sleep analysis.
- **Not a coaching platform.** No personal trainer content.
- **Not a hardcore sim.** Stats are abstract (STR/END/AGI/FLX/STA), not PT-grade metrics.
