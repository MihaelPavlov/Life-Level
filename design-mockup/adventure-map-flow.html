INTERACTION SUMMARY
1
Open map — see player position, fog of war, mini stat cards at bottom
2
Tap a zone — slide-up panel with details, bonus, enemies, rewards, Travel CTA
3
Tap conversion button — full movement table slides up, shows today's progress
4
Reach new zone — fog lifts with animation, discovery banner, sparkles, +XP popup
5
Swipe up mini-cards — weekly calendar, streak count, map km progress expanded
KEY UI PATTERNS
📌 Bottom sheet for zone info, movement card, weekly stats — slides up, dismissible
🌫 Fog of war via layered SVG gradient rects + radial gradients over undiscovered areas
📍 Player dot with animated pulse rings, always rendered above everything else
🎯 Zone tap target is larger than the visual circle (touch area = 2× radius)
✨ Discovery burst = radial glow + expanding SVG rings + CSS sparkle particles
